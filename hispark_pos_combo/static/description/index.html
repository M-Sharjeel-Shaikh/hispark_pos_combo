How It Works

Step-by-step process for using the Combo & Custom Deal Builder in POS.

Step 1: Install the Module

Install the “POS – Combo & Custom Deal Builder” from your Odoo Apps.

Step 2: Mark Combo Products

Enable the “Combo” or “Make Your Own” option on product configuration.

Step 3: Build in POS

When selling, easily select combos on the POS screen.

Step 4: Half-and-Half Option

Provide customers with dynamic options such as half-and-half combinations directly from the POS.
